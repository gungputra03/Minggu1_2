Batur Mountain

Situated in the cool mountainous region of Kintamani in north-east Bali, Mount Batur is the island’s second highest volcano (after Agung). Travellers come for sunrise trekking, jeep safaris, and a taste of rural Bali. Infrastructure is still basic in this relatively remote destination, so don’t expect the facilities (or visitor numbers) of the South.

But if raw nature and off-the-beaten-track adventure is your thing, Mount Batur is a must. And don’t forget to pack a jumper!
